Frequently asked questions

How does iCloud sync work?

Your notes are stored in your own iCloud account and sync automatically across your Macs signed in to the same Apple Account. Keeping notes small (a gentle ~5,000-character guide) helps them sync quickly and reliably.

What's free, and what's Inkholt Pro?

Inkholt is free for up to 20 documents. Inkholt Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited documents. Your existing notes are never deleted or hidden — the limit only stops creating new ones.

I bought Inkholt Pro on another Mac. How do I restore it?

Open the upgrade screen and choose “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to buy.

Where is my data? Is it private?

Your notes live only in your own iCloud (Apple's private CloudKit database). The developer cannot see, collect, or transmit them. See our Privacy Policy.

Can I export my notes?

Yes — export any note to HTML, offline HTML, Markdown, or PDF from the File menu.
