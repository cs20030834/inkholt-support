Privacy Policy

Inkholt · Effective June 7, 2026

Inkholt is designed to keep your writing private. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Your notes

Notes you create in Inkholt are stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in to iCloud, in your own private iCloud account (Apple's CloudKit private database). This data is synced by Apple between your devices. The developer of Inkholt cannot access, read, collect, transmit, or sell your notes.

Data we collect

The developer collects no personal data. Inkholt contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. The app does not create developer accounts and does not transmit your content to any server operated by the developer.

Purchases

Inkholt Pro is an in-app purchase handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple processes the payment; the developer does not receive or store your payment information.

iCloud

iCloud sync is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy. You can turn iCloud syncing on or off in macOS System Settings.

Children

Inkholt is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
